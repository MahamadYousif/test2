Apple iPhone XR Summary

Apple iPhone XR smartphone was launched in September 2018. The phone comes with a 6.10-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 828x1792 pixels at a pixel density of 326 pixels per inch (ppi) and an aspect ratio of 19.5:9. Apple iPhone XR is powered by a hexa-core Apple A12 Bionic processor. It comes with 3GB of RAM. The Apple iPhone XR runs iOS 12 and is powered by a 2942mAh non-removable battery. The Apple iPhone XR supports wireless charging, as well as proprietary fast charging.

Price : 450 $
